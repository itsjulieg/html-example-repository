TV Shows and Movies

One of the best things to do during the fall season is to get comfy and watch a good TV show or movie. Since there are so many movies and TV shows to enjoy during the fall season, we will list our top picks below, so get your warm blankets, pumpkin flavored drinks, and fuzzy socks ready!

TV Shows

Classics

One of the most famous TV Shows for the fall season is Gilmore Girls, which is about a mother-daughter bond between Rory Gilmore and her single mother Lorelai Gilmore and their life in their town called Stars Hollow. This show includes themes such as family dynamics, relationships, ambition, and financial troubles.

Other TV Shows that are perfect for the fall season include Friends, Twilight, Pretty Little Liars, and Glee.

New Releases

Stranger Things is another popular TV Show on Netflix that was released on July 15, 2016, and since then, it has been a major hit! It is set to release its new and final season coming out on November 26, 2025.
